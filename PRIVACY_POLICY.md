# K Music Bot - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** September 3, 2025  
**Effective Date:** September 3, 2025

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how K Music Bot ("the Bot") created by MDA Team ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot service.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Discord Server ID:** To manage server-specific music queues
- **Discord User ID:** To process commands and manage user preferences
- **Voice Channel Information:** To connect and play music in voice channels
- **Command Usage:** To process music requests and bot functionality

### Data We DO NOT Collect
- Personal messages or voice communications
- Email addresses or personal contact information
- Voice recordings or audio data
- Browsing history outside of Discord
- Payment or financial information

## How We Use Information

We use collected information solely for:
- **Bot Functionality:** Processing music commands and managing queues
- **Server Management:** Maintaining separate music queues per Discord server
- **User Preferences:** Remembering language settings and volume preferences
- **Error Logging:** Improving Bot stability and fixing technical issues

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Practices
- Data is stored temporarily in memory for Bot operation
- No persistent storage of user data or conversations
- Queue data is cleared when the Bot disconnects from voice channels
- Logs are automatically deleted after 7 days

### Security Measures
- All data transmission is encrypted via Discord's secure channels
- No data is shared with third parties
- Access to Bot infrastructure is restricted to authorized team members
- Regular security audits and updates are performed

## GDPR & KVKV Compliance

We are committed to protecting your privacy under GDPR and Turkish KVKV regulations:

### Your Rights
- **Right to Access:** Request information about data we process
- **Right to Deletion:** Request removal of your data from our systems
- **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Request your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object:** Object to certain data processing activities

### Legal Basis for Processing
- **Legitimate Interest:** Providing Bot functionality and improving service
- **Consent:** Language preferences and optional features
- **Contract Performance:** Delivering requested music services

## Third-Party Services

### YouTube Integration
- The Bot uses YouTube's API to search and stream music
- YouTube's own privacy policy applies to content streamed
- We do not store or control YouTube content

### Discord Platform
- The Bot operates on Discord's platform
- Discord's Privacy Policy applies to platform interactions
- We only access necessary Discord API functions

## Data Retention

- **Temporary Data:** Cleared immediately after command execution
- **Queue Data:** Removed when Bot leaves voice channel
- **Log Files:** Automatically deleted after 7 days
- **User Preferences:** Stored in memory only, cleared on Bot restart

## International Data Transfers

- Data processing occurs within secure cloud infrastructure
- All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws
- Appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers

## Children's Privacy

- The Bot is not intended for children under 13
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- Parents can contact us to request removal of child's data

## Changes to Privacy Policy

- Material changes will be announced through appropriate channels
- Continued Bot usage constitutes acceptance of updated policy
